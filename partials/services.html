Financial Services

Mission Backers knows what it is like to have to track and process cash with limited staff to manage accountability, track accounts payable, manage accounts receivable, and ensure timely processing of participant vouchers while running monthly accounting statements to ensure appropriate reporting for the Board of Directors, and we can do the same for you!

Mission Backers uses Financial Edge by Blackbaud which is an accounting software designed with nonprofits in mind. The system offers tools and data required to keep organizations on track and all budget information in one place. Whether preparing financial statements for the board, compiling reports, or providing expense details to leadership, Financial Edge software makes accurate, actionable reports possible.

Our certified public account will provide regular consultation with members to make sure questions are answered and concerns are addressed.
